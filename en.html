Methodology

    Scope and how to read this

    Coverage is a curated strategic market map of orbital launch supply nodes in the U.S., Europe and China. It emphasizes programs that either deliver orbital launch today or matter for 2027-2030 competitive structure. It is not a census of every micro-launch startup.

    Use 2026 deliverable kg to understand who can really move mass this year. Use 2030 potential kg to compare option value if reusable vehicles mature. Use single-launch payload to see raw vehicle class, which is often the least useful number on its own.

    Large state fleets are modeled as a single fleet node when splitting variants would create false precision or double-count capacity. Those nodes can still be explored in detail, but they are intentionally not mixed with individual vehicle programs one-for-one.
